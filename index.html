A Great Place to be Present...

Saratoga Springs Retreat Center nurtures personal and collective transformation, celebrates diverse communities, and sustains the natural beauty and resources of our land.

Saratoga Springs Retreat Center

is nestled in a private 260-acre pristine valley, in the beautiful rolling hills of Northern California, 2 1/2 hours West of Sacramento and 2 1/2 hours North of the San Francisco Bay Area.

Experience our retreat center which is available for groups of 25 or more, seeking privacy, serenity and a closeness to nature that inspires our guests to say: "I feel at peace and present here."

Everyone is welcome and invited to experience Saratoga Springs for ‘Exclusive Group Use’. Many weeknights and winter weekends are available for overnight stays for groups less than 25. For groups of 25 or more, we offer exclusive, private use on a first come, first serve basis of our facilities and amenities in our 260 acre valley. We provide a serene venue for retreats, workshops, family weddings and reunions, and gatherings of friends.

The Center: Facilities and Amenities

Main Lodge

Historic two-story lodge with commerical kitchen, dining room, large meeting area and conference room, and overnight lodging upstairs for up to 28 people.

Heart Lodge

A beautiful 2,400 sq ft multi-purpose room with radiant heated floors & stone fireplace. Perfect to use for your workshops, yoga, meditation, or receptions.

Guest Cabins

5 large guest cabins with bathrooms and both private & shared rooms. 8 small single room cabins The Cabins can sleep between 50-80 guests.

Yurt

30-foot diameter yurt multipurpose space. Tucked away in our campgrounds, this yurt is adaptable to your needs. Equipped with heating and cooling for year-round use.

Pool and Hot Tub

Our refreshing swimming pool is a great way to cool off on hot summer days. The heated spa is available for year-round use and is a favorite with our guests. Showers and restrooms are available in the bathhouse.

Campgrounds

Camping for up to 250 is available, with showers and bathrooms in our swimming pool and hot tub area. Our 260 acre private valley offers plenty of shade amongst the oak trees to find a cozy campspot!

The Landscape

Saratoga Springs provides a safe and supportive place for you to follow your own path and nurture your body, mind and soul. It is a place where you can simply be present. This sacred site has been used for centuries by Native Americans to connect with nature and their inner-self. Most everyone who visits Saratoga Springs leaves with a renewed spirit and improved health just from walking our grounds.

Here you'll find plenty of hiking opportunities from short strolls to vigorous 2-3 hour climbs. Views of Clear Lake, the Blue Lakes and even Lake Mendocino to the west are at hand. There are also opportunities to see some of the distinctive flora and fauna of our humble valley.

Weddings at Saratoga Springs

Plan your wedding or commitment ceremony at Saratoga Springs Retreat Center. You, along with your family and friends, will enjoy the luxury of exclusive use of our lodging, reception hall, kitchen, and amenities for the entire weekend.

We offer affordable wedding packages. Our Wedding Coordinator is gracious, experienced and ready to provide you with a list of local celebration vendors such as florists, professional photographers, bakeries, caterers, rental companies, and additional nearby lodging for larger groups.

One of the most subtle, but practical considerations for celebratory groups, is the safety of your guests. No one need drive home when the festivities are winding down. Saratoga offers ample guest lodging both in our Main Lodge and cabins.
